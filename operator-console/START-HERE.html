RMF Commander — Operator Console

An interactive concept demonstration of AI-assisted RMF portfolio management: continuous ATO visibility, smart alerting, POA&M tracking, and human-gated AI documentation support.

v0.4 PILOT  ·  5-MINUTE SELF-GUIDED DEMO

To open the demo

1
Double-click RMF-Commander-Operator-Console.html It opens in your web browser. Nothing to install, no account, no network connection required. Nothing is sent from your computer.
2
Look for the gray "DEMO DATA" badge in the top bar It confirms you are viewing built-in sample data. Every system, finding, and name in the demo is fictitious.

Suggested 5-minute tour

1
Portfolio dashboard The opening screen shows every system as a tile with a live countdown to ATO expiration, color-coded by urgency (green / amber / red), plus open POA&M counts and scan freshness. This is the "single pane of glass" for an ISSM.
2
Click any system tile Opens the system detail page. Use the tabs to see its POA&Ms and its NIST SP 800-53 control compliance status with evidence references.
3
Alerts (left sidebar) The smart alert engine: 90/60/30-day ATO warnings, POA&M slips, and stale-scan notices — surfaced automatically instead of tracked in spreadsheets.
4
POA&Ms (left sidebar) The portfolio-wide POA&M tracker with severity, owner, and milestone at a glance.
5
AI Assistant and Briefings (left sidebar) The AI documentation assistant drafts control statements grounded in NIST SP 800-53 — and every AI output waits for human review before it is used. Briefings generates an executive summary of portfolio risk on demand. The Audit Log shows how every AI interaction is recorded for accountability.

What this demonstrates

  • Continuous visibility — ATO status, POA&M posture, and scan freshness for the whole portfolio in one view, instead of scattered spreadsheets.
  • Proactive alerting — expirations and slips surface themselves before they become emergencies.
  • AI with accountability — drafting help for RMF documentation with a mandatory human-approval gate and a full audit trail.
